# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be kind. Be generous with your assumptions about other people. We're here to build something cool
and learn things together — not to win arguments or make anyone feel small.

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in Omni-SAG a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Examples of behavior that helps

- Being welcoming and patient, especially with newcomers.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what's best for the project and the community.
- Assuming good faith.

## Examples of behavior that doesn't

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments; personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome advances.
- Sustained disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to the maintainers via
a [private GitHub report](https://github.com/rupivbluegreen/omni-sag/security/advisories/new) or by
contacting a maintainer directly. Reports will be handled discreetly and in good faith. Maintainers
may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code, and may ban
repeat or severe offenders.
